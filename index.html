penlock

State-of-the-art seed phrase backup.

Penlock is an open-source, cryptographically secure, printable paper-computer. It guides users through secret-splitting their BIP39 seed phrase without an electronic device.

Getting things done

Generate a Seed Phrase

Generate a 12-word seed phrase using real-world, provable randomness.

Time required:
  • Learning: ~10 minutes
  • Execution: ~20 minutes

-> Open the Guide

Split a Seed Phrase

Split a seed phrase into 3 (encrypted) shares. Any two of these shares can be used to recover the original seed phrase, but a single compromised share reveals nothing about it.

Time required:
  • Learning: ~30 minutes
  • Execution: ~60 minutes / 12 words

-> Open the Guide

Recover a Seed Phrase

Recover a seed phrase from 2 Penlock shares belonging to the same set.

Time required:
  • Learning: ~20 minutes
  • Execution: ~40 minutes / 12 words
